Joseph Breeden, Ph.D.

I am a PhD graduate from the University of Michigan. My research focuses on safety-critical control theory, with an emphasis on spacecraft applications and multi-agent systems. My research aims to make space systems more reliable, autonomous, and scalable. See highlights from my research below.
